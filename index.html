02 Core Services

Engineering & MEP Systems

"We provide comprehensive utility management, from PEA high-voltage incoming power to complex internal distribution. Our water management strategies focus on resilience, advising on triple-source security (Provincial Waterworks, Deep Well, and Supply Trucks). We implement advanced treatment and filtration systems before allocation, alongside sophisticated soil and waste management. To support sustainability goals, we offer Grey Water recycling systems for landscape irrigation. For climate control, we specialize in high-efficiency VRV/VRF systems tailored for space-sensitive luxury projects. Our team is deeply familiar with the stringent Fire & Life Safety requirements of international brands, having successfully delivered projects for IHG, Marriott, and Hyatt.".
